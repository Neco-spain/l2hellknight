Эти классы доступны после первого выбора профессии. Класс Mystic состоит из двух подклассов - Wizards и Healers. Класс Warrior: Warrior, Human Knight, Rogue, Gladiator, Paladin, Warlord, Dark Avenger, Treasure Hunter, Hawkeye, Elven Knight, Elven Scount, Temple Knight, Swordsinger, Plainswalker , Silver Ranger, Palus Knight, Assassin, Shillen Knight, Bladedancer, Abyss Walker, Phantom Ranger, Orc Raider, Orc Monk, Destroyer, Tyrant, Scavenger, Artisan, Bounty Hunter и Warsmith. Класс Mystic: Human Wizard, Sorcerer/ss, Necromancer, Warlock, Elven Wizard, Spellhower и Phantom Summoner.
Класс Healer: Cleric, Bishop, Prophet, Elven Oracles, Elven Elder, Shillen Oracle, Shillen Elder, Orc Shaman, Overlord и Warcryer.
