Gatekeeper Ragara:
Magically-empowered movement was not granted to male Kamael in the beginning. That was never an issue until the Kamael began to venture onto the continent, where the vast distances made it much more advantageous. We Kamael were forced to borrow the power from other races if we wished to benefit from it.
We finally appealed to the magicians of the Ivory Tower, who installed several teleportation strongholds in areas frequented by the Kamael. Beginning adventurers may even use the service for free.
